Who We Are

Education Is the Most Powerful Weapon

Takane Academy was born from a simple belief: every South African child deserves access to quality education regardless of their socioeconomic background. Since 2015, we have been breaking barriers, building bridges, and nurturing the next generation of leaders, innovators, and change-makers.

We work hand-in-hand with schools, families, and local government to create sustainable, community-led educational ecosystems across South Africa.

Inclusivity: We reach learners in underserved urban and rural communities.

Excellence: We hold ourselves and our students to the highest standards.

Ubuntu: We believe in the power of community and collective growth.

What We Do

Programs Designed to Transform

Our programs address education holistically — from academic support to life skills, mental wellness, and digital readiness.

📚
Academic Support

Targeted tutoring, mentorship, and curriculum support for Grades 8–12 in Maths, Science, and English to boost pass rates and university access.

Core Program
💻
Digital Skills

Coding bootcamps, digital literacy workshops, and technology access initiatives bridging the digital divide for township youth.

Technology
🌱
Youth Leadership

Leadership camps, civic education, and entrepreneurship training to cultivate confident, responsible community leaders of tomorrow.

Leadership
🎨
Arts & Culture

Music, drama, and visual arts programs that celebrate African heritage while building creativity, confidence, and emotional intelligence.

Creative Arts
🧠
Wellness & Counselling

On-site psychosocial support, group therapy sessions, and wellness workshops ensuring students thrive mentally and emotionally.

Wellbeing
🤝
Bursary & Funding

Connecting deserving students with bursaries, university application support, and financial aid to make tertiary education a reality.
